Best 5 Saloon Cars of 2024

  1. Audi A4
  2. Mercedes Benz
  3. BMW 3 Series
  4. Toyota Crown
  5. Toyota Mark X

Audi A4

This car is classified as a small luxury, all wheel saloon car. The interior of the car is as great as the exterior. It offers comfortable, smooth, quiet drive making it the most sought luxuey car.

Mercedes Benz

Mercedes Benz is best known for its up to date advanced technology which include face detection, parking sensors, lane assist and a reverse camera. The engine type options cary for both petrol and diesel.

BMW 3 Series

If you're looking for a luxurious, sporty sedan, the BMW 3 series offers exactly that. They include the BMW 330i and M340i. The 3 series have several engine options including a turbocharged 3 liter six cylinder and a turbocharged 2.0 liter.

Toyota Crown

This car is a combination of luxury and comfort.It comes equipped with with either a 2.5 liter engine or a 3.5 liter engine for the hybrid models. The car has an upgraded safety features which is supported by pre crash system.

Toyota Mark X

The mark X is a five seater middle sized sedan car. It is available in both rear and all wheel drive. The engine is a 2.5 liter V6 and a 6 speed automatic transmission. Besides getting good value for your money, you also get low cost of maintainance.
